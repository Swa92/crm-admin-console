Project = Sales line · the container

Projects

Each project is a sales line defined by parameter metadata. A project can have zones (Div/ZM layer) or be zone-less & pan-India (e.g. Digital). When a customer lands in the client DB, the engine matches these parameters to route the lead. Projects can be created or dismantled anytime – the S0–S7 workflow never changes.

Customer auto-assignment

S0–S7 lead workflow

The pipeline is fixed and decoupled from team structure. Whichever project or zone a lead belongs to, it runs the same seven stages. Reorganize teams → change projects, zones & parameters, never the pipeline.

Inside → Direct handoff & re-plug rule

Inside Sales works a lead through S0–S2. At S2 the city is checked:

Inside Sales · S0 → S1 → S2
City ∈ 22 focus cities → Re-plug to Direct Sales → zone by city · stage continues from S2
New / non-focus city → Stays in Inside Sales · continues S3 → S7
ℹ️ General re-plug rule: if a lead matches Direct's parameters (focus-22 city) at any stage, it re-plugs into the Direct zone list – without resetting the stage. Parameters are evaluated continuously, not only at creation.
